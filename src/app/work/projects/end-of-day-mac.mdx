## What this is

A menu bar app that runs in the background and closes your configured work apps when you tell it the day is done. No more manually quitting Slack, Chrome, VS Code, and the rest.

## What's in it

- **Menu bar residence** — Runs quietly in your macOS menu bar
- **Configurable apps** — Choose which apps to close at end of day
- **Custom day picker** — Set your specific work days (not every day is a work day)
- **Analytics** — Track how often you end the day, which days you work late
- **Launch at login** — Starts automatically when you log in

## Why it matters

The goal isn't to stop working — it's to create a boundary. Having a physical ritual (clicking "end of day" in the menu bar) makes it easier to actually walk away.

## Stack

- Swift / SwiftUI
- AppKit for menu bar integration
- UserDefaults for local persistence
